About this Project
Analysed 10,000 Rows using SQL, Power BI and Power Query to evaluate Revenue and Profitability performance using data-driven insights.
Key Insights
•	Total Revenue of 5.7M, indicating a strong sales performance during the period.
•	January contributed the highest in Sales and Profit performance.
•	Books and Clothing have the highest pricing compared to other categories.
•	Average Order Value (AOV) stands at 571.92, indicating a moderate basket size per transaction.
•	Returns show a fluctuating trend over time, indicating inconsistent customer return behaviour.
•	Channel and segment pricing analysis shows Silver has the highest pricing, while Gold is the lowest.
•	Revenue and profit show steady overall growth with minor monthly fluctuations.
•	Defective and wrong items are the reasons for the majority of product returns.
Tools Used
SQL: identified missing values and replaced them with mean, median, or mode.
Power Query: Data Transformation.
Power BI: Data Visualisation.
Recommendations
•	Focus on Top-performing products and categories to improve overall profitability and revenue.
•	Improve performance of low-performing categories by marketing campaign, discounting schemes and combo offers.
•	Optimise Books and Clothing pricing strategy to balance demand and profitability.
•	Address returns caused by defective and wrong items by improving quality checks, packaging accuracy, and order fulfilment validation.
•	Offer cash discounts to improve liquidity and encourage faster customer payments.
•	Focus on improving loyalty gold segment through targeted rewards, personalised offers, and retention programs.
•	Study the impact of returns on profit and implement corrective actions to reduce profit erosion from returned orders.

Overall, improving product mix, reducing returns, optimising pricing across segments, and strengthening loyalty programs will significantly enhance profitability, stability, and long-term business growth.
